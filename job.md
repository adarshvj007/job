To find genuine PHP Laravel developer jobs, you need to look where the high-quality companies post. Because Laravel has such a dedicated community, there are specific "hubs" that are much better than generic job boards like Indeed or Monster.

Here is a breakdown of the best places to find genuine Laravel roles:

### 1. The "Gold Standard" Laravel Boards
These are the most reliable because companies usually have to pay a significant fee to post here, which filters out low-quality or "ghost" job listings.

*   **[LaraJobs](https://larajobs.com/):** This is the official Laravel job board. It is the #1 place for high-quality, genuine Laravel positions (Remote and On-site).
*   **[Laracasts Jobs](https://laracasts.com/discuss?q=jobs):** Check the discussion forum on Laracasts. Often, companies looking for developers who follow "the Laravel way" post here.
*   **[Laravel Jobs on Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23laravel%20jobs&src=typed_query):** Follow the hashtag `#LaravelJobs` or accounts like `@LaraJobs`.

### 2. Startup & Tech-Specific Boards
Laravel is very popular in the startup world. These sites focus on tech-heavy companies.

*   **[Wellfound (formerly AngelList)](https://wellfound.com/):** This is the best place for startup jobs. You can apply directly to founders or CTOs. Search for "Laravel" or "PHP."
*   **[We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/):** Excellent for finding high-paying remote Laravel roles from US and European companies.
*   **[Remote OK](https://remoteok.com/):** Similar to We Work Remotely, very tech-focused.

### 3. Professional Networking (The "Hidden" Market)
*   **LinkedIn (Optimized Search):** Don't just look at the "Jobs" tab. Use the search bar for **Posts** with keywords like: *"Hiring Laravel developer"* or *"Looking for a PHP dev."* This helps you find recruiters posting directly to their feeds.
*   **Laravel Discord/Slack Communities:** Join the [Laravel Discord](https://discord.com/invite/laravel). There is usually a `#jobs` or `#freelance` channel where real developers and managers post openings.

### 4. Vetted Platforms (If you are Senior level)
If you have 3+ years of experience and can pass a technical test, these platforms match you with high-paying clients:
*   **Toptal:** Very hard to get into, but once in, the jobs are elite.
*   **Gun.io:** Specifically for high-end freelance and full-time engineering roles.
*   **Lemon.io:** Specifically focuses on matching PHP/Laravel devs with startups.

---

### How to stand out (Tips for Laravel Devs)
To get hired in a competitive market, ensure your profile shows you know the modern **"Laravel Ecosystem"**:

1.  **Mention the "TALL" or "VILT" Stack:** Employers currently look for **TALL** (Tailwind, Alpine.js, Laravel, Livewire) or **VILT** (Vue.js, Inertia.js, Laravel, Tailwind).
2.  **GitHub is your Resume:** Ensure you have at least one clean project on GitHub showing:
    *   Proper use of Migrations and Seeders.
    *   Service Classes or Action Classes (to show you don't put all logic in Controllers).
    *   A test suite (Pest or PHPUnit).
3.  **Certification:** If you want an extra edge, the **[Official Laravel Certification](https://certification.laravel.com/)** is recognized by many high-end agencies.
